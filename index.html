STUD AMS: Student’s Attendance Monitoring System for STI College Novaliches with QR Code and Cloud Computing (Title Proposal)

The Student Attendance Monitoring System supports online attendance tracking with just a click, doing away with the pen and paper attendance marking method. Teachers can manage and monitor student attendance data using this module from desktop and mobile devices. Students can scan their QR Code in front of QR Code readers with the help of a student monitoring system, which automates the entire procedure and records attendance in the attendance management module. Since teachers do not have to spend their valuable time marking attendance, their workload is reduced.
